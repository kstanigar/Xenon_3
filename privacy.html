Privacy Policy

Standing Tiger Engineering & Development · Last updated: June 15, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Standing Tiger Engineering & Development ("Standing Tiger," "we," "us," or "our"), based in Texas, USA, collects, uses, and protects information when you play NON-X ("the Game") at nonx.standingtiger.com.

By playing NON-X you acknowledge this policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the Game.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following categories of information:

  • Player display name — entered voluntarily when submitting a leaderboard score
  • Score data — your game score at the time of submission
  • Gameplay behavior data — detailed in-game events and metrics collected via Google Analytics 4 (only with your consent), including:
    • Game sessions started, won, and lost
    • Level reached, phase (Green/Red/Purple), and death location
    • Boss encounter and defeat events
    • Powerup collection by type and phase
    • AI difficulty tier adjustments (tier values -3 to +3, direction of change)
    • Score multipliers based on difficulty tier and movement scheme
    • Session duration in seconds
    • Movement control scheme selected (keyboard or mouse)
    • Music setting and A/B test group assignment
    • Number of games played and site visits (session counting)
    • Replay behavior (whether you played again after game over)
    • Whether an Instagram handle was provided with a leaderboard submission
    • Analytics version tag (used for filtering data by feature release)
  • Device and platform data — device category (desktop/mobile/tablet), IP address, and browser identifiers collected by Google Analytics 4
  • reCAPTCHA signals — interaction data used to detect bots and protect the leaderboard (collected via Google reCAPTCHA v3)
  • Cookie and consent preferences — stored locally in your browser (localStorage) to remember your choices
  • Firestore identifiers — database record IDs and timestamps associated with leaderboard entries

We do not collect passwords, payment information, or government-issued identifiers. Ko-fi handles all donation payment processing independently.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Leaderboard operation — to display your display name and score on the public leaderboard
  • Game analytics — to understand how the Game is played and improve it (only with your consent)
  • Bot and fraud prevention — to protect the leaderboard from abuse via reCAPTCHA v3
  • Security — to monitor for and respond to abuse or unauthorized access

We do not sell your personal data. We do not use your data for targeted advertising.

3. Analytics Toggle & Cookie Consent

NON-X provides two controls for managing your analytics consent:

Cookie Consent Banner
Shown on your first visit. Clicking Accept enables Google Analytics 4 tracking. Clicking Decline prevents GA4 tracking. Your choice is saved to localStorage under the key nonx_consent.
Analytics Toggle (Settings)
Visible at all times on the main menu. This toggle lets you opt out of analytics tracking at any time — even after previously accepting the consent banner. Your preference is saved to localStorage under the key nonex_analytics. Turning this off stops all GA4 event tracking for your session and all future sessions until re-enabled.

When analytics is disabled (either by declining the banner or turning off the toggle), no GA4 tracking events are sent. The only exception is the toggle action itself — a single event is always recorded when you change the analytics setting so we can accurately count opt-out requests.

4. Leaderboard Data is Public

When you submit a leaderboard score, your display name and score are publicly visible to all players of NON-X. Do not use your real name or personally identifying information as your display name.

Leaderboard data is stored in Google Cloud Firestore. Standing Tiger may reset leaderboard entries at any time to remove cheated scores or for maintenance.

5. Analytics Data Pipeline & Public Dashboard

When you consent to analytics, your gameplay data flows through the following pipeline:

Game → Google Analytics 4 → Google BigQuery → AWS Lambda API → Public Dashboard
  • Google Analytics 4 (GA4) — receives gameplay events and custom dimension data directly from the Game in your browser. Your individual session data is stored in GA4 under Google's infrastructure. Google Privacy Policy
  • Google BigQuery — GA4 exports data to BigQuery for complex analytics queries that GA4 alone cannot perform (such as per-session AI tier progression). Data is stored in Google Cloud. BigQuery Security
  • AWS Lambda API — a custom API deployed by Standing Tiger on Amazon Web Services queries the GA4 Data API and serves aggregated data to the analytics dashboard. Individual session data is not stored by the Lambda; it queries and passes through aggregated results only.
  • NON-X Analytics Dashboard — aggregated, anonymized gameplay statistics are displayed on a publicly accessible dashboard at kstanigar.github.io/non-x_analytics/. The dashboard shows population-level metrics (win rates, death rates, boss defeat rates, difficulty tier distributions, etc.) and does not display individual player records or identify specific users.

6. Other Third-Party Services

NON-X also uses the following third-party services. Each has its own privacy policy:

  • Firebase / Cloud Firestore — stores leaderboard entries in Google Cloud. Firebase Privacy Policy
  • Firebase App Check + reCAPTCHA v3 — protects against bots. As of April 2, 2026, Standing Tiger is the data controller for reCAPTCHA data; Google processes it on our behalf. Google Privacy Policy · Google Cloud DPA
  • Ko-fi — donation button. Clicking it navigates you to Ko-fi's platform; Ko-fi handles all payment processing. Ko-fi Privacy Policy
  • Amazon Web Services (AWS) — hosts the Lambda API that processes analytics queries. AWS Privacy Policy

Google and Amazon may process data in the United States and other jurisdictions. By using the Game, you acknowledge this international data transfer.

7. Data Retention

  • Leaderboard entries — retained indefinitely unless you request deletion or we reset the leaderboard
  • Google Analytics data — retained for 14 months by default (Google's standard retention period)
  • Local preferences — stored in your browser's localStorage until you clear browser data

8. Your Rights

Under the Texas Data Privacy and Security Act (TDPSA) and, where applicable, the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), you have the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of personal data we hold about you
  • Delete — request deletion of your leaderboard entry or other personal data
  • Correct — request correction of inaccurate personal data
  • Opt out of tracking — use the Analytics toggle or cookie consent banner at any time
  • Opt out of data sales — we do not sell personal data; this right is satisfied by default

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the address below. We will respond within 30 days.

9. Children's Privacy

NON-X is intended for users aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected data from a child under 13, please contact us immediately and we will delete it.

If you are a parent or guardian and have concerns about your child's use of NON-X, contact us using the information below.

10. Data Security

We implement reasonable security safeguards to protect your information, including Firebase's built-in security rules, CloudFront HTTPS enforcement (TLS 1.2+), and Firestore access controls. No system is completely secure; we cannot guarantee absolute security of data transmitted over the internet.
